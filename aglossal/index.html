Shadow Portrait

The AI reads the patterns of what you suppress — not your words, but the weight of them — and writes you a portrait. Not a diagnosis. A mirror.

Example Shadow Portrait

You carry more than most people know how to carry. The patterns in what you hold back — the weight of them, the frequency, the way some feelings return again and again — speak to someone who feels everything deeply and trusts the world with very little of it.

There is a particular kind of pain that lives in your unsaid things. Not the sharp kind, but the persistent kind — the kind that stays in the background of everything and shapes how you move through rooms, through conversations, through silences.

That voice deserves to be heard. Beginning with you.

Privacy

Built on one principle:
what you write here is yours.

The Shadow Portrait AI receives only anonymized behavioral metadata — never your actual words.
