Common questions

What is Aglossal exactly? ▾

Aglossal is a private vault for your unsaid things — the messages you typed and deleted, the voice memos you recorded and threw away, the things you almost said. You write, hold, and optionally release them when you're ready. It is not a social app, a messaging app, or a therapy service. It is a private space that is entirely yours.

Is my vault data private? Who can read it? ▾

Nobody but you. All vault entries are encrypted with AES-256 and stored only on your device. We cannot read your vault entries — we have no access to them. They are protected by Face ID or Touch ID. They never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to release them via a feature like Direct Delivery.

Can I cancel a Direct Delivery after sending? ▾

Yes — you have a 10-minute window after sending to cancel. After that window closes, the delivery is sent and cannot be recalled. You can cancel from the release screen by tapping "Cancel delivery" before the countdown expires.

How does Shadow Portrait work? ▾

Shadow Portrait generates a personal psychological reflection using Claude (Anthropic's AI). It reads only anonymized behavioral patterns from your vault — total entry count, emotion tag distribution, revision frequency, and time-of-day writing patterns. Your actual words never leave your device. The portrait is a creative mirror, not a clinical assessment. It requires at least 5 vault entries.

How do I manage or cancel my Premium subscription? ▾

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel or change your plan: open iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Aglossal. You can cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid being charged. Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com — we cannot issue refunds directly.

I lost access to my account. How do I sign in? ▾

If you registered with email, use the "Forgot password" link on the sign in screen — a reset email will be sent to your address. If you used Sign in with Apple, sign in with the same Apple ID. If you are still unable to access your account, email us at support@embur.world and we will help.

How do I delete my account and all my data? ▾

Open Settings within Aglossal → scroll to "Danger zone" → tap "Delete all my data" → type DELETE to confirm. This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all associated data from our servers and your device. This action is irreversible. Your vault entries are also wiped. If you have pending Time Capsules, they will be cancelled and not delivered.

What is the age requirement? ▾

Aglossal is rated 17+ on the App Store and intended for users aged 17 and older. The content of the app — personal emotional reflection, unsaid messages, release features — is not appropriate for minors. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 17.

Is Aglossal a mental health or therapy app? ▾

No. Aglossal is a personal reflection tool, not a clinical service. It has not been evaluated by any medical or psychological regulatory authority. It is not a substitute for professional mental health care. The crisis keyword detection feature surfaces resources but does not provide clinical assessment or contact emergency services on your behalf.

The app says it detected something concerning. What should I do? ▾

Aglossal includes on-device keyword detection that can surface mental health resources. These are provided for convenience only. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis line directly. Do not rely on this app for crisis support.
