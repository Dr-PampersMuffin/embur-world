Quick answers

Yes — AIDD is 100% free, forever. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no freemium upsells. Our mission is to make AI-powered health intelligence accessible to every person on Earth at no cost. This is a fundamental promise we will never break. AIDD is funded and developed as a public health initiative by Embur Inc.

AIDD is a clinical decision support tool for educational and informational purposes only. It does NOT provide a medical diagnosis and is NOT a replacement for a licensed healthcare professional. AIDD analyses are powered by peer-reviewed medical literature and AI vision, but they cannot examine you physically, order tests, or take responsibility for medical decisions. Always consult a qualified doctor before making any health decision. AIDD is best used to prepare informed questions for your healthcare provider.

Yes. All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Your images are stored on Cloudinary's secure servers. EXIF location data is stripped from every photo upon upload. Your name and email are never sent to any AI engine — only anonymized symptom data is processed. We never sell your data. We comply with GDPR, HIPAA (aligned), COPPA, and Kuwait's PDPL. Read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.

AIDD is designed to be family-safe. Children under 13 (in COPPA regions) or under 16 (EU/UK) require verifiable parental consent before use. For minors, all analyses automatically recommend in-person professional evaluation, emergency alerts have lower thresholds, and data retention is minimized. Parents or guardians must register accounts on behalf of children under the applicable age of digital consent in their jurisdiction. For concerns about a minor's use of AIDD, contact privacy@embur.world.

AIDD covers four kingdoms of life: 🧬 Human — skin conditions, eye conditions, symptoms, disease identification using PubMed, WHO ICD-11, DermNet NZ, and more. 🐜 Insect — species identification, danger level, venom status, disease vector assessment using Kindwise insect.id (14,000+ species). 🌿 Plant — plant health, disease diagnosis, treatment protocols using Kindwise plant.id (400+ diseases). 🐾 Animal — breed identification, health conditions, veterinary guidance using The Cat API, Dog API, and PubMed veterinary literature.

AIDD uses GPT-4o vision (one of the world's most advanced vision AI models) cross-referenced against 37 million PubMed articles and specialized databases. Every report includes a confidence score — photo-only analyses are capped at 70% confidence maximum by design. Accuracy improves significantly when you provide clear close-up photos alongside a detailed symptom description. AIDD is continuously improving. That said, no AI system is 100% accurate for medical assessment — always verify with a professional.

AIDD's safety engine automatically scans every analysis for patterns matching 40+ emergency conditions including chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe allergic reactions, sepsis indicators, and more. If detected, it immediately escalates the report with a prominent emergency alert and local emergency numbers. If you believe the alert was triggered incorrectly, please still take it seriously and consult a medical professional to rule out a serious condition. Your safety is our priority above all else.

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password? and enter your registered email address. A 6-digit reset code will be sent to your inbox within a few minutes — check your spam folder if you don't see it. Enter the code along with your new password. Codes expire after 15 minutes. If you no longer have access to your registered email, contact us at support@embur.world with your full name and we will assist you.

AIDD uses your device's built-in biometric authentication (Face ID on newer iPhones, Touch ID on older models). Your actual biometric data (your face or fingerprint) is processed entirely on your device by Apple's Secure Enclave — AIDD never receives, stores, or transmits your biometric information. We only receive a pass/fail result from your device. To enable biometric login, log in once with your email and password and tick "Remember me." Biometric login will then be available on subsequent sessions.

AIDD is a strictly educational medical tool. Acceptable photos include skin conditions and rashes (covered body areas), eye conditions, wounds, insect specimens, plant leaves and stems, and animals. Sexually explicit content, gratuitous nudity, or any content sexualizing minors is strictly prohibited and grounds for immediate permanent account ban and referral to law enforcement. Our AI system is configured to detect and reject inappropriate content. For clinically relevant areas (dermatology), maintain modesty while capturing the affected area clearly.

In the app, go to Profile → Request Account Deletion. All personally identifiable data (name, email, health profile, diagnosis history, images) will be permanently deleted within 30 days, in compliance with GDPR's Right to Erasure. Anonymized aggregate data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for research purposes. You can also email privacy@embur.world with the subject "Account Deletion Request."
