1Who We Are

AIDD (Artificial Intelligence Disease Diagnose) is a free universal diagnostic application developed and operated by its founding team, headquartered in Kuwait. AIDD is available globally on iOS and Android platforms.

AIDD is a clinical decision support tool — an educational application that uses artificial intelligence to analyze user-submitted images, symptoms, and descriptions to provide informational health guidance across four domains: Human health conditions, Insect identification, Plant health & disease, and Animal & pet health.

3What Data We Collect & Why

Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Full name — to personalise your experience
  • Email address — for account authentication, password resets, and service communications
  • Password — stored as a one-way bcrypt hash (we cannot read your password)
  • Date of birth — to apply age-appropriate safety guidelines in medical analysis
  • Biological sex — to provide sex-specific diagnostic context (optional)
  • Country & city — for regional disease pattern analysis and aggregate statistics
  • Occupation — optional, for contextual reporting
  • Phone number — optional, not shared with third parties

Health Profile (Optional)

If you choose to complete your health profile, we may also collect:

  • Blood type, height, weight
  • Known medical conditions
  • Current medications
  • Known allergies
  • Smoking status and alcohol use
  • Family medical history

ℹ️ This health data is used exclusively to provide you with more contextually accurate diagnostic suggestions. It is never used for advertising, profiling for commercial purposes, or shared with any third party without your explicit consent.

Diagnostic Session Data

Each time you run an analysis, we record:

  • Subject type (human, insect, plant, animal)
  • Your symptom description or text input
  • Severity rating and duration
  • Body region (for human analyses)
  • Uploaded media (images/videos) — stored securely on Cloudinary
  • The AI-generated analysis report
  • Confidence score, severity assessment, and emergency flags
  • Processing timestamp

Technical & Device Data

We automatically collect limited technical information:

  • IP address (used for security and geographic analysis only, not for tracking)
  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Session timestamps

Consent Records

We record your consent choices (terms acceptance, data use consent, camera consent) along with the date and IP address at the time of consent. This is required for regulatory compliance.

4How We Handle Your Images & Photos

🛡️ Core Commitment on Images: All images submitted to AIDD are used exclusively for generating your diagnostic report. We do not sell, license, or commercially exploit your images. All images are stored with AES-256 encryption.

What Images Are Used For

  • Primary purpose: Sent to our AI vision engine (GPT-4o) for visual analysis to generate your report
  • Storage: Stored securely on Cloudinary servers under your account so you can access your diagnosis history
  • Research (only with your explicit consent): Anonymized images may be used to improve AIDD's diagnostic accuracy — only if you ticked the research consent checkbox at registration

Content Standards — No Nudity Policy

🚫 Strictly Prohibited Content: AIDD is a medical educational tool. Uploading sexually explicit content, nudity for non-medical purposes, content that sexualizes minors in any form, or any content intended to harm is strictly prohibited and grounds for immediate account termination and report to relevant authorities.

AIDD is designed for legitimate medical, educational, and scientific purposes only. Acceptable image content includes:

  • Skin conditions, rashes, wounds (covered body parts only, except clinically relevant areas)
  • Eye conditions, oral health concerns
  • Insect specimens for identification
  • Plant leaves, stems, roots showing disease
  • Pet and animal health concerns

Our AI system is configured to flag and reject inappropriate content. Repeated violations will result in permanent account suspension.

Anonymization of Research Images

If you consent to research use, your images are processed through an anonymization pipeline before being used in any research or model improvement context. This process removes or obscures any identifying facial features, personal items, or location data embedded in image metadata (EXIF data is stripped upon upload).

5Camera & Media Library Access

Camera Permission

AIDD requests access to your device camera for the sole purpose of capturing images or videos for medical analysis. We do not access your camera in the background, do not record without your knowledge, and do not transmit any camera feed without an explicit capture action by you.

Photo Library Permission

AIDD requests access to your photo library to allow you to select existing images for analysis. We access only the specific images you select — we do not scan, index, or upload your entire photo library.

Microphone Permission

Microphone access is requested only when recording video for analysis. Audio from videos is not analyzed, stored, or transmitted unless required for your specific diagnostic session.

Face ID / Biometric Authentication

AIDD uses your device's built-in Face ID or Touch ID for optional login convenience. Biometric data is processed entirely on your device by Apple's Secure Enclave — AIDD never receives, stores, or transmits your biometric data. We only receive a pass/fail authentication result from your device.

How to Revoke Permissions

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device settings:

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → [Camera / Photos / Face ID]

Revoking permissions will not delete your existing data but will prevent certain app features from functioning.

6How Our AI Processes Your Information

Our AI Technology Stack

AIDD uses a multi-engine AI pipeline to generate diagnostic reports:

Engine Provider Purpose Data Sent
GPT-4o Vision OpenAI Visual image analysis Image URL only (no personal data)
PubMed API US National Library of Medicine Medical literature search Anonymized symptom keywords
Kindwise insect.id Kindwise Insect species identification Insect image (base64, no personal data)
Kindwise plant.id Kindwise Plant disease identification Plant image (base64, no personal data)
Pinecone Vector DB Pinecone Medical knowledge retrieval Anonymized symptom embeddings

What AI Does NOT Do

  • Our AI does not make definitive medical diagnoses
  • Our AI does not replace a licensed healthcare professional
  • Our AI does not have access to your full personal profile — only anonymized symptom data is sent to AI engines
  • Your name, email, and account details are never sent to any AI engine

AI Safety Guardrails

AIDD implements multiple safety layers on every analysis:

  • Emergency detection: If our AI identifies symptoms matching emergency conditions (chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe bleeding, etc.), the report immediately escalates with emergency services contact information
  • Confidence capping: AI confidence scores are capped based on input quality — photo-only analyses are capped at 70% confidence maximum
  • Mandatory disclaimer: Every report includes a mandatory medical disclaimer regardless of severity
  • Pediatric safeguard: For users under 18, all reports recommend immediate in-person medical evaluation

8Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

Data Type Storage Provider Location Encryption
Account & health data Supabase (PostgreSQL) AWS ap-northeast-1 (Japan) AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
Images & videos Cloudinary Distributed global CDN AES-256 at rest, HTTPS in transit
Medical knowledge vectors Pinecone AWS us-east-1 Encrypted at rest and in transit
App backend Railway US West TLS 1.3, HTTPS enforced

Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256 industry-standard encryption
  • Password security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt (12 rounds) — we cannot read your password
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with 30-day expiry; biometric authentication via device Secure Enclave
  • Access control: Role-based access; staff access to user data is strictly limited and logged
  • Audit logging: All access to user data is logged with timestamps and IP addresses
  • EXIF stripping: Location metadata is removed from all uploaded images upon receipt
  • No third-party analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any commercial tracking tools

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and relevant regulatory authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, as required by GDPR Article 33. Notification will be sent to your registered email address.
